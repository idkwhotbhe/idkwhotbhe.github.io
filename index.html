How to make an Eaglercraft Server In Your own Browser!

Step 1: Go to Replit

Step 2: Press "Fork" and chose a name for your server!

Step 3: Once your console loads, press the green button that says Run

Great! Now you have your server running!

Step 4: Now, to get your server ip, go to the webview and copy anything past the https://

Step 5: Once you've done that, go to any client you have and put in what you've copies and add
EX: wss://replit.idkwhotbhe.repl.co/server

Alright, Now you've made your server! have fun!!!!
